# Product Guide: Voice & Vision Assistant for Blind

## Initial Concept
An AI-powered Voice & Vision Assistant for Blind and Visually Impaired Users.

## Vision and Goals
The Voice & Vision Assistant for Blind aims to bridge the visual gap through AI-powered assistance. By leveraging cutting-edge speech recognition, NLP, and computer vision, this laptop-based software solution acts as an intuitive assistant. It is built to serve a broad spectrum of the visually impaired community, delivering comprehensive situational awareness and interaction capabilities.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Broad spectrum of visually impaired users, encompassing both those with total blindness and those with partial/low vision.
- **Environment:** Users operating via laptops/computers seeking a software-based assistance tool.

## Key Value Propositions
- **Comprehensive Feature Set:** Covers everything from obstacle detection to document reading and internet searches.
- **Ultra-Low Latency:** Optimized for sub-500ms response times for real-time spatial awareness and warnings.
- **Privacy & Offline First:** Local processing, offline caching for QR/AR, and a strictly opt-in, persistent memory storage.
- **Rich Context & Detail:** High-quality contextual descriptions provided by advanced dual-vision models (Qwen-VL) prioritizing safety and accuracy.

## Core Features & Use Cases
- **Outdoor & Spatial Navigation:** Object detection (YOLO/ONNX), edge-aware segmentation, depth estimation, and prioritized micro-navigation cues.
- **Indoor Object & Face Recognition:** Scene analysis, conditional privacy-aware people detection, and environment summarization.
- **Text & Reading:** 3-tier OCR fallback (EasyOCR, Tesseract, MSER) and comprehensive Braille reading capabilities.
- **QR/AR Code Decoding:** Contextual deep-linking with an offline cache.
- **Virtual Avatar Representation:** Tavus virtual avatar integration for video calls, assisting blind users in maintaining professional visual presence with sighted individuals.
